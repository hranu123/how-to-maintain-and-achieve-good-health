Workout Plans You Could Potentially Follow

In the fitness community, there are a variety of different workout plans you could follow and implement into your weekly schedule based on your preference, desired goals/physique and based on the amount of free time you have available. The workout plans include:Push-Pull-Legs (PPL), which organizes the exercises that involve a "pushing" motion, such as bench press, shoulder press and push-ups in a seperate day from the exerices that involve a "pulling" motion, such as tricep pulldown, lat pulldown and barbell row; Calisthenics Workout Plan, which revolves around doing body weight movements, such as push-ups, dips and pull ups; Full-Body Circuit Workout Plan, which involves an exercise for each indidual muscle group for each day you workout; High-Intensity Interval Training (HIIT) Plan, which involves high intensity body weight movements, such as burpees or jump-squats; and a combination of different workout plans you can find more about at: The Best Workout Splits For Every Goal
